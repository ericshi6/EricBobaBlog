Eric's Take on Boba

I moved from Boston to the San Gabriel Valley after college.

“I hear East Coast boba is garbage.”

Well that was rude. I guess West Coast boba drinkers are lacking in personality.

In the 626, boba is not just a drink. It’s a lifestyle. There is no other Chinese diaspora that must define themselves by a sucking tapica up a fat straw.

Search for Boba


Some of my favorite boba places:


TeaDo

Locations: Boston, Philidelphia, Rockville

I’m a little biased here. When I was a student in Boston, I frequently visited this place since it first opened and even worked at TeaDo for two days before I left for California. When I wasn’t selling their boba for school fundraisers, I’d meet here with my friends while we played Jenga and ordered onigiri and takoyaki. It’s become a very popular place since then but the quality remains.

Notable Drinks: Thai Iced Tea, Pikachu, The Hulk

The Alley

Locations: Irvine, Fountain Valley, Las Vegas, Honolulu

Brown sugar! You shoulda heard him just around midnight.

Featured in the Lee Min-ho and Kim Go-eun K-drama The King: Eternal Monarch, The Alley’s brown sugar boba feels like a royal experience. The bottoms of the cups are generously lined with sugar and honey boba. Crème brûlée is also a nice touch.

Notable Drinks: Matcha Brown Sugar Deerioca Milk, Brown Sugar Deerioca Crème Brûlée Milk

Half & Half Tea Express

Locations: Pasadena, Arcadia, San Gabriel

An SGV Classic. Reliable chain with a large selection of flavors and consistent quality. Boba in a fat cup is a 626 staple.

Notable Drinks: Iced Milk with Caramel+Pudding+Honey Boba, Iced Milk Drink w Grass Jelly + Honey Boba, Bo's Coffee + Sea Salt Latte

Tan-Cha

Locations: Irvine, Garden Grove, Rowland Heights

What separates Tan-Cha from a typical boba cafe is their cheese foam. The foam has a thick consistency with just the right amount of sweet and salty. The foam is very versatile and pairs well with sweet or bitter milk teas and light fruit drinks.

Notable Drinks: Uji Matcha with Red Bean, Fresh Grapefruit Slush, Cheezo Grape

Jin Tea Shop

Locations: Pasadena

Have you ever asked yourself if boba can be upscale? The café serves artisan tea, cold brewed tea, milk tea, and organic specialty tea in a tall cup with a modern aesthetic.

Notable Drinks: Butterfly Pea Flower w/ Lemonade, Organic High Mountain Oolong Tea
